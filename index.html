What is a security standard?

    It is a set of criteria organizations can follow to protect sensitive and confidential information.

    Without these standards, companies wouldnt have anything preventing others from taking valuable and dangerous information from them or their customers

How you can stay safe

BEFORE you submit any private data to a website, check its privacy policy. The privacy policy will tell you how the data will be used and whether or not it will be shared with other users. If the policy cannot be found, you can contact the company with questions about their policies.
